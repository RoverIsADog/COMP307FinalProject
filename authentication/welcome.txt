<h1 class="auth-h1"><b>Welcome to the New McGill SOCS TA Management System!</b></h1>
<h2>Students</h2>
<p>
	Students can use this website to rate TAs in courses they are taking.
</p>
<h2>Teaching Assistants</h2>
<p>
	TAs can access and edit information on the courses they are teaching.
</p>
<h2>Teachers</h2>
<p>
	Teachers can manage and edit TAs in their courses.
</p>
<h2>TA Administrators</h2>
<p>
	TA administrators can view and manage TA course assignments.
</p>
<h2>System Operators</h2>
<p>
	System Operators can manage and edit users.
</p>
